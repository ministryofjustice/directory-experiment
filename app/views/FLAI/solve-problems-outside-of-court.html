Solve problems outside of court

Solving issues without having to go to court could save you time and money.

Read Advicenow’s guide to solving legal problems before or instead of going to court.

For example, you may be able to come to an agreement or get an explanation or apology from the person you’re having a problem with.

You could also work with a mediator to try to get compensation.

A mediator is an independent professional who will help you and the person you’re having a problem with talk through the issues and come to an agreement.

Find out more about mediation and how to find a mediator.

You might be able to get help with the costs of family mediation through legal aid. Find out if you’re eligible for legal aid.
