Affordable advice and support

You can get affordable legal advice and support from many different government departments, charities and professional advisers.

Getting lower-cost help usually depends on:

  • the problem type
  • how far it has progressed
  • your financial situation

Legal aid

You may be eligible for legal aid, depending on your financial circumstances and the type of case you have.

Check if you qualify for legal aid

Other advice and support

If you do not qualify for legal aid, there are other options where you can get support at a reduced cost or even for free.

Find free legal advice in your local area

A solicitor may also be able to take on your case as part of their pro bono work. Find a pro bono lawyer on the LawWorks website.
