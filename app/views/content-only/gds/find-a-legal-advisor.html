Find a legal advisor

Legal advisors can help with a wide variety of legal problems, depending on what they specialise in.

Solicitors

You can get advice from a solicitor, but their support is not always free. A solicitor can help with:

  • Contentious legal work – resolving disputes between two or more parties, usually in a court or tribunal.
  • Non-contentious legal work – dealing with the legal aspects of a client's business or personal matter, for example managing a company merger, or making a will.

Find a solicitor through The Law Society if you live in England or Wales

Barristers

A barrister can help you with many legal issues, for example, by providing advice on your legal rights, drafting legal documents for you and representing you in a court or tribunal.

Find a barrister in your area through the Bar Council if you live in England or Wales.

You may be able to see a barrister without seeing a solicitor first if you live in England or Wales.
