Resolve problems outside of court

If you have a dispute with a person or organisation, it is often possible (and even preferable) to resolve it without going to court.

Alternative dispute resolution (ADR) is the name for a range of options for resolving legal problems, often without going through the court process.

Your legal advisor could suggest ways to sort out your problem without going to court. They can give you advice on your rights and options, or to help you negotiate with the person or organisation you have a problem with.
