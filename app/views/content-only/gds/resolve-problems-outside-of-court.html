Solve problems outside of court

Solving issues without having to go to court could save you time and money.

Read Advicenow's guide to sorting out legal problems before or instead of going to court. It covers the services available for solving different types of problems, information about making complaints, and the costs involved.

You may be able to come to an agreement, get an explanation or apology, or be paid compensation through working with a mediator. A mediator is an independent professional who will help you talk through the issues and come to an agreement that everyone is happy with. Find out more about mediation and how to find a mediator.
