Resolve problems outside of court

Going to court can be a long and stressful experience. Resolving your dispute out of court may help you save a lot of time and money.

Your legal adviser can advise you on your rights and options available, or help you find the best dispute resolution method for your case.

'Dispute resolution' is the term used for the most common methods of resolving problems in civil, family and administrative areas. These include mediation, conciliation, negotiation and arbitration.

Here are some useful guides to get you started:

  • Sort out your legal problem before or instead of going to court (Advicenow)
  • Why use ADR? Pros & cons (Advice Services Alliance)
  • Introduction to forms of alternative dispute resolution (Lease)
