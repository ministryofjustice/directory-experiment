Dealing with problems early

If you start dealing with your problem early, you may be able to resolve it more easily and cheaply than going to court.

You could save yourself a lot of time and money by doing your own research and looking at all the options available to you. The longer you leave it, the worse it may become.

Types of legal problems

There are many different kinds of legal problems and they all need to be handled in specific ways.

Find out more about these and how they all need to be handled in specific ways.

These are the types of problems covered by this guide:

  • Benefits
  • Debt, money and tax
  • Employment
  • Housing and homelessness
