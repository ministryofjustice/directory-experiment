Going to court

You may need to go to court if you have not been able to resolve your problem by other means.

Here are some guides about the different UK courts and what to expect when you need to go to one of them:

  • Learn about the different UK courts
  • Going to civil court
  • Going to criminal court

Representing yourself in court

You have the right to speak for yourself in court without a solicitor or other legal professional.

You should prepare thoroughly if you choose to do this, to ensure the best possible outcome.

Learn about representing yourself in court (GOV.UK)
