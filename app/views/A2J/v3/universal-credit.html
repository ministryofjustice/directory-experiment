For a progress update, please either ask in your journal or contact the Universal Credit helpline on 0800 328 5644.

You will only receive an additional amount within your Universal Credit award if you are deemed to have limited capability for work and work-related activity. This is where you are deemed unable to work or do any work preparation. It amounts to an extra Â£341.92 per month. You can normally not receive this element if you have earnings that are 16 the National Minimum Wage unless you are also receiving a benefit such as PIP.
