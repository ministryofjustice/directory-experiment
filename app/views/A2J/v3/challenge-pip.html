Consider challenging your PIP decision

You mentioned you were unhappy with the award you received. You may want to consider challenging the decision by asking for a mandatory reconsideration. This is where you ask the DWP to look at the decision again.

You can submit additional evidence at this point. You should do this in writing preferably by completing theCRMR1 Formwithin 1 month of the date on the decision letter you will receive. You should save a copy of the decision letter as you may need to refer to it.

Should you receive PIP, your Universal Credit will not be reduced by any amount. If you need support with challenging the decision, please contact us further.
