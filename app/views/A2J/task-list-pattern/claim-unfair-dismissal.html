Make a claim for unfair dismissal if you didn't resign

You would be entitled to all the protections from unfair dismissal due to your length of service. As you have worked for over two years, your employer would need to show you have dismissed for a fair reason. It isn't black and white and will depend on your individual circumstances.

They would also be required to follow the correct dismissal process.

Actions

  • Write a formal letter of grievance
  • Contact ACAS to work with your employer to solve the problem
  • Book an appointment with an advisor if you want to make an employment tribunal claim
